Our Trip Types

Home > Trip Type

Centre-Based Holidays

Our centre based adventures are ideal if you want to control the pace of your holiday and explore one region in more depth. On these trips we carefully research a region and find a hotel, guesthouse or other accommodation to use as a base for the duration of the holiday. We then organize walks and other activities from the base, allowing you to discover all the region has to offer while returning to your comfortable accommodation at the end of each day.

Private Departures

Our new Private Departures are designed for people who prefer the flexibility of travelling independently. Based on the same itinerary as the small group tour, Private Departures give you the freedom to choose your own departure date and simply enjoy the adventure with your family, your partner or your friends.

Private Group Holidays

Have you ever thought of arranging your own private group adventure? If you have around six or more people in your party we can organise a private group departing on a date to suit you, customised especially for you with all the activities and experiences you want, working to your proposed budget.

Self-Guided Holidays

An YCT Travel self-guided trip is really a holiday on your own terms. Whether you are travelling as a couple, alone or with friends our range of self-guided trips ensures maximum flexibility for your holiday. You can depart on any day of the week, get up when you want, cycle or walk at your own pace and take a break whenever and wherever you like.

Small Group Trips

Unlike conventional group holidays, where you’ll see large groups and flag waving guides, our small group adventures are informal and flexible, allowing you plenty of time to do your own thing. On every tour there’s a balance between group activities and personal time for you to explore independently or relax as you like.

Tailormade Holidays

YCT Travel tailor-made holidays are ideal for those who prefer to travel at their own pace, or to explore places not found on other itineraries. Our travel experts will suggest ideas to complement your own and help organise the best holiday to suit your interests, time and to match your individual budget. Our website has some guideline itineraries and prices to help you get started, but remember, everything is flexible.
